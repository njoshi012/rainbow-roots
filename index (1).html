Our Story

A Place Where Every Child is Celebrated

At Rainbow Roots, we believe that childhood is a once-in-a-lifetime journey — and every step of it deserves joy, wonder, and nurturing care. Founded with a vision to bring world-class early childhood education to Kalyan, we've created a space where little ones don't just learn, they thrive.

Our approach weaves together play-based learning, emotional intelligence, creative exploration and academic readiness — because we know that the roots planted in these early years grow into a lifetime of confidence.

Got Questions?

Everything Parents Ask Us

We know choosing a preschool is one of the most important decisions you'll make. Here are honest answers to the questions we hear most.

What age groups does Rainbow Roots accept?
+

We welcome children from 1.5 years to 8 years old across our Playgroup, Nursery, Junior KG, Senior KG, Daycare and Activity Center programs. Each program is carefully designed for that specific developmental stage.

How do you ensure my child's safety?
+

Safety is our highest priority. We have 24/7 CCTV monitoring of the entire campus, a secure entry system where only authorized adults can collect children, first aid trained staff in every classroom, daily sanitization and a full-time security guard. Parents receive real-time notifications for attendance and pickup.

What is the teacher-to-child ratio?
+

We maintain a maximum ratio of 1 teacher to 12 children for Playgroup and Nursery, and 1:15 for KG classes. This small batch size ensures every child receives the individual attention and care they deserve.

Do you offer a free trial class?
+

Yes! We offer a complimentary trial class for every prospective student. We believe your child should love Rainbow Roots before you commit. Simply book a school visit and we'll arrange a trial class as part of the process.

What curriculum does Rainbow Roots follow?
+

We follow an internationally inspired, activity-based learning curriculum that blends play-based pedagogy with structured academics. Our approach is rooted in research on early childhood development and is designed specifically for Indian children and cultural contexts.

What are your timings and fees?
+

Our school hours are Monday to Saturday, 7:30 AM to 1:30 PM for the main programs, with extended daycare available until 7:00 PM. For detailed fee structure and admission packages, please contact us directly — we're happy to walk you through all options.

How do you keep parents updated?
+

We use a parent communication app where you receive daily attendance updates, meal reports, photos and activity updates throughout the day. Teachers also send personal updates and parents can message teachers directly. We also have WhatsApp group updates and monthly parent-teacher meetings.

Where exactly is Rainbow Roots located?
+

We're located at Ground Floor, Pehelwan Chowk, Triveni Bliss, 10 Umbarde Road, Opposite Triveni Classic, Chanakya Nagar, Khadakpada, Kalyan, Maharashtra – 421301. Easily accessible by road, with parking available nearby.
